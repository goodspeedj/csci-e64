European countries by Population

The following map of Europe shows differing population densities with the most populous countries a darker shade and the least populous countries a lighter shade.

Interactivity Design

The map employs a mouse over effect that will highlight the border of the country by making the stroke color darker and thicker. The tool tip will also display the country name as well as the population of that country. I decided on the tool tip instead of placing the data directly in the borders of the country to keep the map cleaner and less cluttered. Countries that are not part of Europe were colored a light shade of gray so that they faded into the background and the European countries pop out to the user.

Visual Queries

The map shows the total population of European countries and quickly shows the most populous vs. least populous countries. The map does not provide population density data. To get fuller picture of the population of Europe a more advanced version of this map might include a toggle that would switch between overall population and population density.
