European countries by Population

The following map of Europe shows differing population densities with the most populous countries a darker shade and the least populous countries a lighter shade. The most populous country is Russia (143,369,806), which has a small corner of land in Europe. The least populous country is Iceland (321,857).

The map employs a mouse over effect that will highlight the border of the country by making the stroke color darker and thicker. The tool tip will also display the country name as well as the population of that country. Countries that are not part of Europe were colored a light shade of gray so that they faded into the background and the European countries popped out to the user.
